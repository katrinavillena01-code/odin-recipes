Garlic chicken

Home

Garlic chicken is a flavorful dish that combines tender pieces of chicken with the rich aroma of saut√©ed garlic. The chicken is usually marinated in a mix of garlic, soy sauce, and spices before being cooked to golden perfection, giving it a delicious balance of savory and slightly sweet taste.

When cooked, the garlic caramelizes and creates a fragrant sauce that coats the chicken beautifully. This dish can be enjoyed with steamed rice, mashed potatoes, or vegetables, making it a simple yet satisfying meal for lunch or dinner.

ingredients

  • olive oil
  • garlic
  • bread crumbs
  • parmesan cheese
  • chicken

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Heat olive oil and garlic in a small saucepan over low heat until warmed, 1 to 2 minutes. Pour into a shallow bowl.
  3. Combine bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese in a separate shallow bowl.
  4. Use tongs to dip chicken breasts in olive oil-garlic mixture, then coat evenly in bread crumb mixture. Transfer coated chicken to a shallow baking dish.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink and juices run clear, 30 to 35 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
